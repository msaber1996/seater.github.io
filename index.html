ABOUT US

Seater has taken the integration of technology, and transportation to a new level. With technological solutions spanning all potential services, and needs, Seater has got you covered!

Seaters services extend beyond just daily commuting routes, and intercity travel. It also facilitates the transportation to and from major events, and football matches.

As well as catering to the needs of private business and groups that need to coordinate mass transportation for various reasons, including but not limited to: Conferences, Weddings, Trips, Outings, Travel, Limousine Service, and more.
